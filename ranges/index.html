Slab Suave

My typeface is called "Slab Suave." Suave, meaning charming, sophisticated, and elegant, is the perfect word to describe the look I wanted for my typeface. The letterforms in "Slab Suave" have ascenders and descenders that range depending on the letter which adds to the overall elegance and make it look more decorative and fun. Its standard slab serif and decorative calligraphic elements combine to create the perfect concoction.
